Computer Vision in Medical Imaging

This page consists of three models built by Medical Imaging Team.

  • Pneumonia Classifier will predict whether the person is having pneumonia.
  • Brain Tumor Classifier will predict which type of tumor is present from in the brain MRI scan.
  • Brain Tumor Segmentation Model will highlight the tumor affected areas from brain MRI scan.

Upload the various scans and the predction done by the model will be displayed.
