Internship Project Demo - Medical Imaging Team

This page consists of three models built by Medical Imaging Team.

  • Pneumonia Classification Model will predict whether the person is having pneumonia or not from Chest X-ray.
  • Brain Tumor Classification Model will predict the type of tumor from brain MRI scan.
  • Brain Tumor Segmentation Model will highlight the tumor affected areas in brain MRI scan.


Upload the various scans and the predction done by the model will be displayed.
